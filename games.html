How to Play

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

👥 Game Modes

  • Single Player: Play against an unbeatable BOT opponent (you are X, BOT is O). The BOT uses the minimax algorithm to make perfect moves.
  • Two Players: Play with a friend on the same device (Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O).

🎮 How to Play

  1. Select your game mode from the home menu
  2. Click on any empty cell to place your mark
  3. Take turns with your opponent (or the AI)
  4. Try to get three in a row while blocking your opponent
  5. The game ends when someone wins or the board is full (draw)

📊 Stats

  • Wins: Number of games you've won
  • Losses: Number of games you've lost (against AI)
  • Draws: Number of tied games
  • Double-click on stats to reset them

🎯 Tips

  • In single player mode, the BOT is unbeatable - your best result is a draw!
  • Control the center for better chances of winning
  • Always watch for opponent's potential winning moves
